## 7. Drawer Architecture Recommendation

Recommended architecture:
- portal-based fixed drawer

Rejected for first implementation:
- in-shell drawer
- shell-clipped overlay
- section-owned drawer

Reasoning:
- current `MobileShell` uses `overflow-hidden`
- header is sticky and already layered
- frozen sections should not become z-index negotiation surfaces
- focus trap and body scroll lock are safer outside section flow

## 8. Portal / z-index / Scroll-lock Strategy

Recommended layering:
- `GlobalHeader`: `z-50`
- drawer backdrop: `z-80`
- drawer panel: `z-90`

Recommended scroll-lock strategy:
- apply scroll lock at the document/body level during drawer open
- do not rely on `MobileShell` for lock behavior
- keep focus inside drawer while open
- restore trigger focus on close

Recommended portal strategy:
- drawer renders outside section tree
- drawer does not inherit clipping from `MobileShell`

## 9. MobileShell overflow-hidden Risk

Current shell:
- [components/layout/mobile-shell.tsx](/Users/zinwang/Workspace/project/ZENOVELL-V4-PIMPZ/PROJECTS/ZENOVELL-PIMPZ-V4-Active/components/layout/mobile-shell.tsx:1)
- inner wrapper uses `overflow-hidden`

Risk:
- any in-shell overlay or animated drawer can be clipped
- sticky + overlay combinations may behave inconsistently inside the shell
- future blur/shadow/backdrop layers may be visually cut off

Architectural decision:
- preserve `MobileShell` as-is during extraction
- solve drawer via portal, not shell mutation, in the first pass
