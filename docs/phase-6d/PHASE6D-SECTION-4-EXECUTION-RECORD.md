## Lessons Learned

Initial governance lessons from Section 4 preparation:

- Strategic UX authority must be separated from original visual authority
- Scope Lock is necessary to prevent assumption-based UI work
- Product discovery sections need stronger decision framing than image-replication blueprints usually provide
- Implementation safety improves when handoff documents are explicit and concise
