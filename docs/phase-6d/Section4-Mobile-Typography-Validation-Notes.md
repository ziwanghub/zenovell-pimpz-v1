# PHASE 6D — SECTION 4 MOBILE TYPOGRAPHY VALIDATION NOTES

## What Changed

- Thai font rendering quality was improved
- Intro heading and subtitle typography were stabilized
- Fit, difference, support, and trust text were refined at small widths
- `360` and `375` received the primary readability-focused treatment

---

## Why

These changes address the final presentation-layer blockers identified by independent review:

- Thai intro typography stability
- `360` readability
- `375` readability
- small Thai support text comfort

The refinement was intentionally limited to typography behavior only.

---

## Expected Readability Improvement

- cleaner Thai intro rendering
- calmer first-scan impression
- easier fit-line comprehension on the smallest widths
- clearer difference-line scan speed
- more comfortable support text without increasing visual competition
- no hierarchy regression at `390 / 414 / 430`
