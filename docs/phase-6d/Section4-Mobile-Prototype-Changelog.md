## Scope

This changelog summarizes presentation-layer prototype changes relative to the current runtime.

## Changelog

### 0. Intro Typography Stabilized

- The opening heading was reduced and given more breathing room
- Thai rendering was shifted toward a calmer, more stable first impression
- The intro now releases attention into the grid more gradually

### 1. Card Meaning Layer Reordered

- Product fit was elevated to earlier visual attention
- Title and difference now support faster recognition before detailed confirmation

### 2. Benefit Layer Reduced In Dominance

- Benefits were demoted from mini-reading blocks to quiet confirmation chips

### 3. Image-to-Meaning Balance Improved

- Images remain premium and recognizable, but no longer dominate interpretation as strongly

### 4. Commitment Zone Clarified

- Price and CTA now read more clearly as the decision-completion layer

### 5. Trust Layer Quieted

- Trust was preserved but treated more as reassurance than as a second dense content block

### 6. Bottom CTA Role Clarified

- Bottom CTA was positioned as a fallback consultation path rather than a competing primary action

### 7. Small-width Focus Increased

- Prototype was designed from smallest-width pressure first rather than from the most comfortable width first

### 8. Small-width Card Breathing Improved

- `360` and `375` cards received more internal breathing room
- Meaning layers were separated more clearly before commitment layers

### 9. Thai Meaning Layers Quietly Rebalanced

- Fit, difference, and support text were rebalanced so recognition stays ahead of supporting detail

### 10. Post-grid Recovery Increased

- The trust-to-bottom-CTA transition was given more separation to reduce visual compression after comparison

### 11. Thai Typography Rendering Stabilized Further

- Intro and in-card Thai text were re-rendered with higher visual stability
- The refinement focused on calmer premium perception rather than changing copy or hierarchy logic

### 12. Small-width Readability Tightened Toward Freeze Quality

- `360` and `375` received another pass on text spacing and content separation
- Fit, difference, and support layers were tuned to reduce reading friction while preserving all approved content

### 13. Responsive Consistency Strengthened

- Small-width screens were brought closer to the quality level of `390+` without changing grid, CTA, or product order
