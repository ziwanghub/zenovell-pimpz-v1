# Ownership Matrix Pattern

## Purpose
Make "who is responsible for what" explicit and prevent diffusion of responsibility.

## When to Use
- Multiple layers or teams involved.
- High risk of ambiguous ownership leading to future revisions.

## When NOT to Use
- Trivial single-owner systems.

## Structure
Table with columns: Concern | Owner | Responsibility | Never Does

## Example
See Ownership Matrix in Blueprints.

## Anti-pattern
"Shared ownership" without clear rules.

**Status**: Mandatory in Blueprint Standard v2.
